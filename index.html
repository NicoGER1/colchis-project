History of Colchis Kingdom

In the heart of Asia, between the Caucasian Mountains and the Black Sea, lay Colchis, a kingdom steeped in legend and magic. Founded by the King Aeëtes, Colchis thrived with the secrets of alchemy and the Golden Fleece. When Jason and the Argonauts sought the Fleece, they faced trials and the aid of Aeëtes' daughter, Medea. Despite war and envy, Colchis endured, a bastion of knowledge and mystical arts. Its story intertwined with gods and heroes, leaving an indelible mark on Asia's history.
